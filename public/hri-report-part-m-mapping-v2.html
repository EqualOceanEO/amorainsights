🇨🇳 China's Structural Advantage

China's supply chain dominance is not just about cost—it's about ecosystem density and iteration speed:

  • Manufacturing Ecosystem: 70%+ of global robot component suppliers located within 500km radius in Yangtze Delta and Pearl River Delta
  • Iteration Speed: Hardware iteration cycles 2-3x faster due to proximity to suppliers and manufacturing partners
  • Cost Curve: 30-50% cost advantage at current volumes; advantage widens to 65% at 100K+ scale
  • Supply Chain Resilience: 80%+ localization rate reduces geopolitical risk exposure

🇺🇸 US Strategic Vulnerabilities

US players face structural challenges in scaling manufacturing, despite AI leadership:

  • Manufacturing Gap: No domestic ecosystem for precision mechanical components; 100% import dependency for gearboxes
  • Cost Disadvantage: US manufacturing costs 3-5x higher for equivalent components; erodes margin at scale
  • Supply Chain Risk: Heavy reliance on Asian suppliers creates vulnerability to trade policy shifts
  • Time-to-Scale: Building manufacturing capability requires 3-5 years; China players already at 10K+ unit scale
