Key Findings
🎯 The Winner
Unitree leads with 8.9/10, driven by exceptional Operations score (8.8) from 5,500+ units deployed. Their IPO filing reveals 60% gross margin — proving robots can be a profitable business.
⚖️ The Trade-off
China dominates Operations (manufacturing & deployment), US leads in Mastery (talent density) & Advancement (algorithm depth). The gap is narrowing in both directions.
⚠️ The Risk
High Mastery + Low Operations = "PPT Robot" risk. Several well-funded US startups score 8.0+ on talent but <6.0 on actual deployment — a dangerous disconnect.
