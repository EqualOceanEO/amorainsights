Technology Advancement — Enabling Technologies & Breakthroughs

Humanoid robots represent a deep fusion of mechanical engineering, materials science, sensor technology, and AI large models. After 2023, "Embodied Intelligence" transitioned from academic concept to commercial validation stage, with four major technology pathways evolving in parallel.
